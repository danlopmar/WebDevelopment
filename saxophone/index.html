All About the Alto Saxophone

Alto Sax Facts

  • Invented by Adolphe Sax in the 1840s
  • Common in jazz, classical, and concert band
  • Tuned in E-flat
  • Usually made of brass
  • One of the most popular beginner saxophones

How the Alto Saxophone Works

  1. The player blows air into the mouthpiece.
  2. The reed vibrates against the mouthpiece.
  3. The vibration travels through the saxophone.
  4. Pressing keys opens and closes tone holes.
  5. This changes the pitch of the sound.

Famous Alto Sax Players

  • Paul Desmond
