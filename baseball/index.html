All About Baseball

Baseball Facts

  • 30 teams in Major League Baseball
  • 9 players on the field per team
  • Games are 9 innings
  • Played on a diamond-shaped field
  • Very popular in the United States, Japan, and Latin America

How Baseball Works

  1. The pitcher throws the ball toward home plate.
  2. The batter tries to hit the ball.
  3. If the batter hits it, they run to first base.
  4. Players try to advance around all four bases.
  5. If they reach home plate, their team scores a run.
